Tech stack
3D engine: Three.js + React Three Fiber + Drei (already widely used in your codebase patterns)
Models: glTF 2.0 with PBR materials, one per product family — T-shirt (front+back), Hoodie (front+back+hood), Mug (cylindrical wrap), Cap (curved bill + crown), Tote, Polo
Texture compositing: HTML5 Canvas → THREE.CanvasTexture, redrawn on every layer change at 2K resolution
Print zones: UV-mapped decals per garment area (chest, back, sleeve, etc.) with safe-zone bounds
Fabric simulation: simple normal map + roughness for cotton/polyester realism. Skip cloth physics (overkill, kills mobile perf)
